My favorite food is pav bhaji,

Below is the list of all ingridients required to make pav bhaji

  • vegetables
    1. Potatoes
    2. Lauki
    3. Capsicum
    4. Onions
    5. Tomatoes
  • spices
    1. Pav bhaji masala
    2. Garam masala
    3. Amchur (mango powder)
  • Bread (pav)
  1. boil the vegetables
  2. Mesh all vegetables together into the the bowl
  3. saute unboiled onions and capsicum etc in a pan until golden brown
  4. Add Garam masala than pav bhaji masala
  5. add boiledm vwetables with the fried spices
  6. Mix it well than let it boil
  7. garnish it with coriander leaves & chopped onions on the bhaji

meal is ready now serve & enjoy with your Bread (pav)
